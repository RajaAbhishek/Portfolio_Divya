Background

We addressed user frustration and low conversion rates by adding a prominent "Book Appointment" button on the landing page, which significantly improved the user experience and increased appointment bookings.

Timeline

4 weeks

Tools

Figma & Miro

Collaborators

Problem Statement

One user challenge of not displaying a "Book Appointment" option prominently on a landing page is that it can lead to frustration and confusion for users who visit the page with the specific intent of scheduling an appointment. Here's a breakdown of the challenge:

  1. Missed Opportunity for Conversion
  2. Increased User Effort
  3. Perception of Inaccessibility
  4. Competitive Disadvantage
  5. Loss of Trust and Credibility
